VISION

San Roque Parochial School, Inc. is a Catholic Institution which envisions to develop Filipinos who are Maka-D,yos, Maka-Tao, Maka-Bayan, Maka-Kalikasan at Hindi Makasarili. (PCP II Acts and Decrees No.636)


MISSION

In order to achieve this vision, San Roque Parochial School, Inc. aims to develop the students for their:

  • Academic excellence and responsible leadership;
  • Awareness and concern for all who were deprived of the benefits of justice and charity;
  • Dedication in bringing peace and unity;
  • Promotion of pro-life program of the Church;
  • Preservation and conservation of the cultures and traditions and of the nations natural resource.

OBJECTIVES

  • To foster in the students a sound spiritual life thought religious instructions, personal guidance, and encouragement to frequent regular participation at Holy Mass;
  • To inculcate Gospel values and the values and the development of a good Filipino based on the abiding in God and the genuine love of country;
  • To train young citizens in the exercise of their rights, duties, and responsibilities in a democratic society and to develop moral character, personal discipline, civic conscience, and vocational efficiency;
  • To develop basic understanding about the Philippine culture as an essential requisite in attaining national consequences and solidarity;
  • To develop functional literacy in the vernacular and in English as basic tools for their learnings.

PHILOSOPHY

San Roque Parochial School, Inc. believes that our school is a community in mission for evangelization in the service of the less privileged of the society. It is directed to serve that local community in particular and the nation in general by transforming the youth of today who would be the leaders in the future through a holistic Christian-based learning that would prepare them for higher education, vocational skills and the necessary upbringing needed to gain employment.
